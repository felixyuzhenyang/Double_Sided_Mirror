# Reply Quality Rubric

## P0: Must Pass

- Accuracy: statements align with available policy citations.
- Clarity: citizen can identify first action immediately.
- Accountability: final text is human-authored and approved by staff.
- Safety: include verification reminder for policy-sensitive scenarios.

## P1: Strongly Recommended

- Structure response as:
  1. Understanding of request
  2. Immediate next step
  3. Required documents/information
  4. Timing expectations
  5. Contact or escalation path
- Keep tone respectful and neutral.
- Keep paragraphs short and direct.

## Rewrite Enforcement Heuristic

Use one or more checks before publish:

- Minimum length threshold for final response (for example, 120 characters).
- Similarity threshold between AI draft and final response (for example, block when too similar).
- Required checkbox: "I reviewed and rewrote this response."

## Failure Cases

- Publishing unchanged AI draft.
- Making legal determinations without citation support.
- Omitting next actions or contact path.
