# Staff Workflow

## Step 1: Staff Entry

- Capture `name` or `employee_id`.
- Display active policy notice and AI-assist notice.

## Step 2: Case Selection

- Show inbox of citizen-consented cases only.
- Show status badges: `new`, `in_review`, `responded`.

## Step 3: Review Context

- Read citizen transcript.
- Read AI summary and extracted key facts.
- Confirm issue category before drafting.

## Step 4: Draft and Rewrite

- Generate AI draft reply as a starting point.
- Require human editing in dedicated final-response editor.
- Confirm final response includes:
  - next step,
  - timeline or expected wait window if known,
  - agency contact path,
  - caveats where uncertainty exists.

## Step 5: Publish

- Require explicit publish confirmation.
- Save final response with timestamp and staff identity.
- Expose published response back to citizen timeline.
