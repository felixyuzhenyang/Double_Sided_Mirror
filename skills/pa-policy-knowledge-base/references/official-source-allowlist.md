# Official Source Allowlist

## Core Rule

Allow only Pennsylvania official government sources for policy citations and links.

## Allowed Domain Suffixes

- `pa.gov`
- `state.pa.us`
- `pacodeandbulletin.gov`
- `legis.state.pa.us`

## Notes

- Prefer `https` links.
- Prefer canonical agency pages over mirrors and reposts.
- Treat shortened URLs as untrusted until resolved and validated.
- Reject links that redirect to non-allowlisted destinations.

## Common Examples

- Allowed: `https://www.pa.gov/`
- Allowed: `https://www.dli.pa.gov/`
- Allowed: `https://www.pacodeandbulletin.gov/`
- Blocked: `https://medium.com/...`
- Blocked: `https://example-law-firm.com/...`
