# Safety and Compliance

## User-Facing Notices

- Show that AI output may contain mistakes or outdated interpretations.
- Show that output is informational support, not legal advice.
- Ask users to confirm critical details directly with relevant agencies.

## Data Handling

- Collect only information needed for case processing.
- Avoid storing full API keys, secrets, or unnecessary personal identifiers.
- Redact sensitive fields from logs.

## Consent and Transparency

- Require explicit opt-in before syncing citizen transcript to staff mode.
- Allow citizen to continue without sync.
- Timestamp consent events and include them in audit records.

## Staff Response Integrity

- Require staff to provide a human-authored final response.
- Prohibit publishing raw AI draft as final response.
- Capture both draft and final for internal auditability.

## Citation Integrity

- Restrict source links to Pennsylvania official domains.
- Reject unsupported links from non-official websites.
- Include citation metadata (agency and update date when available).
